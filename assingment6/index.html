My Lego Manifesto

By: Zarin Hawthorne

Everyone is allowed to use Legos until your heart's contents

Everyone remembers how it feels when you let your imagination go wild with Legos, I know I have

Everyone has made that one huge Lego war scene that took time to make, I know I have

Everyone is allowed to collect Legos for nostalgia purposes or just because they like Legos, I know I have

Everyone is allowed to express their ideas through Legos, whether it be a new project or a downtime thing, I know I have

Everyone is allowed to have Legos!
